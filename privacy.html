Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2026

SnS Lens ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal information when you visit our website or enquire about our photography services in Birmingham and across the UK.

Who we are

SnS Lens is a professional photography business based in Birmingham, United Kingdom. You can contact us at hello@snlens.samokuneye.com.

Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry form, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Details about the service you are interested in
  • Any message you send about your project, date, or location

We may also collect basic technical data such as browser type and pages visited through standard website analytics, if enabled.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and booking requests
  • Provide quotes and photography services
  • Improve our website and client experience

We do not sell your personal data.

Legal basis (UK GDPR)

We process your data based on your consent when you contact us, and where necessary to take steps at your request before entering into a contract for photography services.

How long we keep data

Enquiry details are kept only as long as needed to respond, manage bookings, and meet legal or accounting requirements — typically up to 6 years for business records where applicable.

Third parties

Enquiry forms are processed by a secure email delivery service so we can receive your message. Messages are sent to our business inbox. We may also use hosting and analytics providers that process limited technical data on our behalf.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, or object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email hello@snlens.samokuneye.com.

You may also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies. If analytics or embedded content is added in future, we will update this policy.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.
