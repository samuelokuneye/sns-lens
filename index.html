About SnS Lens

Where Vision Meets Craft

SnS Lens was founded on a simple belief: every moment deserves to be seen at its finest. Based in Birmingham, we work with clients across the UK — from intimate studio portraits to destination weddings — with a keen eye for composition, mastery of light, and genuine connection.

Whether it's the quiet tear at a wedding altar or the confident gaze in a boardroom portrait, we approach each session with the same dedication to excellence that defines our mantra. Studio sessions in Birmingham; on-location shoots UK-wide.
